FAQs

What is URL shortening? ▼
URL shortening is a technique in which a Uniform Resource Locator (URL) may be made substantially shorter and still direct to the required page. This is achieved by using a redirect on a domain name that is shorter than the original URL.
How can custom URLs help my brand? ▼
Custom URLs, also known as branded links, use your own domain name instead of a generic one. This enhances brand recognition, builds trust with your audience, and improves click-through rates by making your links more professional and memorable.
Is LinkSphere free to use? ▼
Yes, LinkSphere offers a free "Basic" plan that includes unlimited links and basic analytics. For advanced features like custom domains and A/B testing, you can upgrade to our Pro or Enterprise plans.
Do you offer API access for developers? ▼
Yes, API access is available with our Enterprise plan. This allows developers to integrate our URL shortening and tracking capabilities directly into their own applications and workflows.
